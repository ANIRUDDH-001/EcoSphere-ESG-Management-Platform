# EcoSphere ESG Platform — Demo Run-of-Show

**Value Proposition:** EcoSphere bridges the gap between raw ESG data and actionable intelligence, turning compliance into a strategic advantage powered by grounded AI.

## The 3-Minute Narrative

### 1. The Overview (Dashboard)
- **Action:** Open the app and land on the **Dashboard** as an Admin.
- **Talking Track:** "Welcome to EcoSphere. At a glance, executives see their real-time ESG score—calculated live from underlying activities across all departments. Notice the AI Insight Banner immediately surfacing that the Logistics department is lagging in Governance."

### 2. Deep Dive with Grounded AI (Copilot)
- **Action:** Open the **ESG Copilot** and ask: *"Why is governance low for Logistics?"*
- **Talking Track:** "Instead of digging through tables, we can just ask our AI Copilot. Unlike generic chatbots, this AI is strictly grounded to our database via read-only SQL tools. It tells us exactly why: there are overdue compliance issues."

### 3. Taking Action (Governance)
- **Action:** Navigate to **Governance → Compliance**. Select an overdue issue for Logistics and mark it **Resolved**.
- **Talking Track:** "Let's fix that. By resolving this compliance issue, we're taking direct action on the AI's insight."

### 4. Employee Engagement (Social)
- **Action:** Switch to the **Social → CSR** tab (acting as a Manager). Approve a pending employee participation for a recent CSR activity.
- **Talking Track:** "ESG isn't just about executives; it's about employees. Here, a manager approves a beach cleanup participation, which immediately awards points and XP to the employee."

### 5. Real-Time Impact (Dashboard & Gamification)
- **Action:** Return to the **Dashboard**. Observe the scores have increased. Open the **Gamification** tab to see a new badge unlocked for the employee.
- **Talking Track:** "Back on the dashboard, the scores have already recomputed live. The governance fix and social approval instantly moved the needle. Furthermore, the employee's new XP automatically unlocked a 'Sustainability Champion' badge, driving continued engagement."

### 6. Reporting for the Board (Reports)
- **Action:** Navigate to **Reports**. Click **Generate AI Executive Summary**, then click **Export PDF**.
- **Talking Track:** "Finally, when it's time to report to the board, our single-shot AI generates a grounded executive summary in seconds. We export the final PDF, and we're done."
